Typing Tips & Tricks

Want to improve your typing speed and accuracy? Start with posture. Keep your back straight and wrists relaxed. Don’t look at the keyboard train your fingers to know where the keys are.

Use all ten fingers and try to stay consistent with your hand placement. Practice with purpose: don’t just type quickly focus on hitting the correct keys.

Tools like SpeedTyper help you monitor your progress. Try using it daily, even for just 5 minutes, and you'll see real improvement over time.

Future blog posts will explore ergonomic keyboards, custom typing games, and interviews with top speed typists!
