Join the SpeedTyper Community

SpeedTyper is an open-source project built by people who care about speed, learning, and simplicity. Whether you're a coder, writer, or just someone trying to improve your skills, you're welcome here.

The project lives on GitHub. That's where we track issues, push updates, and collaborate. If you want to help out or just see what's going on behind the scenes, it's the place to start.
